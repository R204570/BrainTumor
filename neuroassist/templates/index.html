How NeuroAssist works

1
3D U-Net segmentation - the model segments the MRI into NCR, ED, and ET regions and calculates volumes, diameter, and lobe involvement.
2
Clinical intake form - the clinician fills a structured form once, and the local model reasons over the verified demographics, symptoms, genomics, pathology, and treatment context together with imaging.
3
WHO 2021 grading - once sufficient data is collected (60%+ completeness), the model generates a WHO-classified diagnostic report.
4
Survival outlook - a validated 0-100 scoring algorithm estimates median OS and flags treatment eligibility.
Important Disclaimer

NeuroAssist is a clinical decision support tool. All outputs must be reviewed by a qualified neuro-oncologist before any clinical action is taken. This tool does not replace clinical judgment.
